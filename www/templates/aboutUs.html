This is a beta mobile version of an ʔayʔaǰuθəm wordlist - an initial step in creating the ʔayʔaǰuθəm/English Online Dictionary. The dictionary is a joint project of the communities who share ʔayʔaǰuθəm as their traditional language: Tla̓amin, Homalco, Klahoose and Comox, and is being developed in collaboration with UBC. The project is supported through a SSHRC Insight grant to Henry Davis, Professor of Linguistics at UBC.
